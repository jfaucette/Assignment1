How does the Calculator work?

The Calculator can be used by clicking on the number buttons and then selecting the operation, and then clicking the equals sign. The calculator will then perform the function you inputted, and display your answer.

Where can I register?

To register, view the Registration Form on the Registration page. You will need to fill out an email and confirm passwords.

Where can I provide feedback?

Feedback can be submitted using the Feedback Form on the Feedback page. Fill out your email and the feedback, then click submit.
